About Us Welcome to Crime Agg! We are a team of passionate developers and data analysts who believe everyone has the right to feel safe in their communities. Our web application and Chrome extension aim to empower individuals and communities by providing real-time access to crime data in their area.

Our mission is to create a more transparent and informed society by enabling our users to track crime data and stay informed about the safety of their neighborhoods by visualizing crime data in an easily digestible format. As a result, we hope to foster a greater understanding of crime patterns and inspire positive change in communities. 


We are committed to providing accurate and up-to-date information and work tirelessly to ensure that our data is reliable and trustworthy. Our team is constantly improving our algorithms and methods to ensure we provide the most comprehensive and accurate crime data available.
  How It Works
1 — View Crime Agg's view feature is designed to allow anyone basic visualizations on crime happening in their area.
Get started
2 — Search With CrimeAgg, users have the flexibility to customize their search by selecting a specific crime type and time frame to view the crime data for. Users can easily input their desired search criteria to access the relevant crime data.
3— Analyze Crime Agg's reports provide more visualizations of relevant crime data in a specific area.
4— Download Crime Agg allows users to download a detailed report in PDF format. This provides users with a permanent record of the crime data for a particular area, which can be useful for reference or sharing with others.
